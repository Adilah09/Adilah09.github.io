About Justin Bieber

Justin Bieber is a Canadian singer and songwriter who rose to fame with his debut single "One Time" in 2009. Known for hits like "Baby," "Sorry," and "Peaches," he has become one of the most influential pop stars of his generation.

Career Highlights

  • 2009: Released debut single "One Time"
  • 2010: Breakthrough hit with "Baby"
  • 2015: Comeback with "Sorry," "Love Yourself," and "What Do You Mean?"
  • 2021: Released chart-topping single "Peaches"

Early Life

Justin Drew Bieber was born on March 1, 1994, in London, Ontario, Canada. Raised in Stratford, he showed a passion for music from an early age, learning drums, guitar, and piano before being discovered on YouTube.
